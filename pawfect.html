About Pawfect

Pawfect Dog Adoption is dedicated to bringing joy and companionship into homes by matching loving families with their ideal canine companions. Founded on the belief that every dog deserves a safe, caring environment, Pawfect partners with local shelters and foster networks to ensure each dog receives the medical care, training, and socialization they need before joining a new family.

Tools

  • Frontend: React with React Router, React-Bootstrap, and axios
  • Auth: Facebook OAuth via react-facebook-login
  • Backend: Node.js + Express with MongoDB (Mongoose), etc.
